Discover your Akan name

Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
Those names are known as Akan names as they are derivered from the ghanian culture.
With this application we will help you know what your akan name would be.
What you have to do is to enter your birthday on the form below.Don't worry if you don't know the actual day of the week.That's our job to find out. and we will suprise you with your Akan name.
